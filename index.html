  • How it works

    • BinReady is a stopwatch that use the BCD clock (Binary-coded decimal clock) to display the time.

    How to read:
    How can i read the time? It's realy easy. The clock is devided in 3 parts: hours, minutes and seconds. Every part has 2 colom's.

    Now you can find the hours, minutes and seconds. Now we go a stap further. Every part has 2 columns, the first columns of each part shows the first numbers of your time and the second columns of each part shows the second number. Every line shows a number and the next line shows the double of the previus number. We start by 1 and go up to 8.

    Try to ready the time on this clock.

    If you answer is 21 hours, 48 minutes and 14 seconds. Your gratulations. Suppose the time is 14 hours, 57 minutes and 6 seconds. You just add these numbers. (see image)
